Financial Inclusion

Of the world's 5 billion adults, over 3.5 billion lack access to basic financial services. Less than 10% of these "unbanked" are served by microfinance institutions. We can do better.

Automation

We automate transactions so that microfinance banks can focus on their core business.

Our solution provides tools used by the major banks (e.g. HSBC, Citi) at as little as 1% of their costs.

We include a robust mobile application for transaction recording and processing in the field, directly increasing labor productivity and reducing operating costs. It is robust to interminent internet and mobile connectivity and builds on a low cost-low energy platform.

Our management platform enables Microfinance Banks to push out new products consistently and seamlessly across far flung locales.

Secure and Reliable

Our cloud platform makes your data accessible and reliable. We make complex technology invisible and secure.

Our Cloud Engine

Our cloud storage and analytics engine enables data to be accessible and reliable, and renders technology invisible.

There are no installations on desktops, configuration of servers, and storage management. These activites divert scarce resources from the core business of providing financial services to the poor and unbanked.

Our cloud engine recognizes emergent economies' embrace of mobile technologies that will enable them to leap ahead.

Becoming Data Informed
